Cancellations vs Deposit Type

Indication on if the customer made a deposit to guarantee the booking. This variable can assume three categories: No Deposit (0) – no deposit was made; Non Refund (1) – a deposit was made in the value of the total stay cost; Refundable (2) - a deposit was made with a value under the total cost of stay.

We can observe a higher dependency between the no deposit variable and if the reservation is cancelled or not.
