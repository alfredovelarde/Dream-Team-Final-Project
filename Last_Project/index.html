Hotel Booking Cancellations

The objective of this project is to predict the hotel booking cancellations taking in consideration different information for a city hotel and a resort hotel, and includes variables such as when the booking was made, length of stay, the number of adults, children, and/or babies, and the number of available parking spaces, among other things.

To accomplish this analysis, we first used the "Hotel booking demand" from Kaggle which contains information on demand data for two different types of hotels: One of the hotels (H1) is a resort hotel and the other is a city hotel (H2). Data comprehend bookings due to arrive between the 1st of July of 2015 and the 31st of August 2017, including bookings that effectively arrived and bookings that were canceled.

After converting the dataset into a dataframe, we reviewed all variables available and converted those categorical variables into numeric in order to analyze the correlations and perform the predictions. It had to be taken into consideration that in some categorical variables like Agent or Company, “NULL” is presented as one of the categories. This should not be considered a missing value, but rather as “not applicable”. For example, if a booking “Agent” is defined as “NULL” it means that the booking did not came from a travel agent.
